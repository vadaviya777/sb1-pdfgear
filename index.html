Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDFgear.cloud completely free to use?

Yes, PDFgear.cloud is completely free to use! We offer all our PDF tools without any cost or hidden fees. You can process your PDF files without creating an account or providing payment information.

How secure are my uploaded files?

Your files are completely secure with us. We use end-to-end encryption for file transfers, and all uploaded files are automatically deleted from our servers after processing. We never store or share your files with third parties.

What's the maximum file size I can process?

Free users can process PDF files up to 50MB in size. This limit applies to individual files for most tools. For the merge tool, you can combine multiple files as long as each individual file is within the size limit.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation is required! PDFgear.cloud is a completely web-based solution. All you need is a modern web browser to access and use our tools from any device with an internet connection.

How do I maintain the quality of my PDFs?

Our tools are designed to maintain the highest possible quality of your PDFs. When using compression, you can choose different levels to balance file size and quality. For conversions, we use advanced algorithms to ensure the best output quality.
